Bio

As a Florida native who now calls North Carolina home, I was swimming in the ocean when I learned to walk. As the years passed I spent most days helping my grandpa on the farm and entertaining my younger siblings as we worked at our parents restaurant. As an adult I have spent most of my career in the medical field, but have recently become very interested in coding. I have always enjoyed conquering challenges and learning new things, which leads me to be interested in a variety of hobbies. Whether it be raising livestock animals, working as a nurse, reading, writing, botany, or the newly added art of coding, I love to keep learning and building new skills.
